Rules

General rules and conducts that apply to the whole server.

General Rules and Code of Conduct



Keep the Lobby and other chatrooms a positive place for chatting and discussion.


Sora has zero tolerance for sexism, racism, pornography, sexual harassment, and bullying.


If you have evidence of power abuse, contact an Administrator (~) as soon as you can.


As we are not an American based server, the First Amendment does not apply.


Please, no Johns.



Tournament Rules



Changing or amending teams in the middle of battles is prohibited.


Counterteaming in a tournament is prohibited.


Failure to follow these rules will result in the disqualification of the abuser.




Staff Rules



Be respectful to everyone, staff and users alike.


Abuse of power is absolutely not tolerated.


Do not 'test' commands on others, unless given the green light beforehand.


Gym Leaders who do not participate in the monthly Elite Four Promotional Tournament

will be kicked out of their position. Additionally, no Gym Leader will be eligible for

Elite 4 promotion unless a certain amount of tournament battles are completed during the month.




Inactivity Clause



Should the league face large amounts of inactivity from the Gym Leaders' front, Frontiers are drafted

into Gym Leader positions. While drafted into Gym Leaders under the Inactivity Clause, Frontiers have to use

their Gym Leader accounts, but still keep their position as Frontier, and the rank they held before.

Frontiers resume their full-time Frontier duties when a new member of the league takes the position they temporarily hold.
